About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of North Texas, working with Dr. Sagnik Ray Choudhury. Before coming to UNT, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), Bangladesh.

My work focuses on natural language processing and large language models, especially how they reason, generalize, and make decisions. I am interested in understanding how these models evaluate open-ended responses and how we can measure and improve their behavior in educational settings. More broadly, I study how AI systems can be evaluated for robustness, generalization, and alignment with human judgment. My goal is to build language-based AI systems that are reliable, interpretable, and useful in real-world learning and assessment contexts.
