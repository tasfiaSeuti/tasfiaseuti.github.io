Research

Benchmark

S-GRADES: Automated Student Response Evaluation

A comprehensive benchmark consolidating 14 datasets across essay scoring and short answer grading tasks, designed to evaluate LLM generalization in diverse educational assessment settings.

Reasoning

LLM Reasoning Strategies for Assessment

Investigating inductive, deductive, abductive, and hybrid chain-of-thought reasoning in models such as GPT-4o-mini, Gemini, and LLaMA across cross-dataset grading tasks.

Simulation

LLM Student Simulation

Exploring how large language models can simulate student behavior and responses in educational contexts, with implications for adaptive tutoring and assessment design.

Interpretability

Chain-of-Thought Faithfulness in Educational AI

Investigating the reliability of chain-of-thought reasoning in LLMs on educational tasks — identifying gaps between stated reasoning and underlying model behavior.
